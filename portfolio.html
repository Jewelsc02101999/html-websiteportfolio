Tecric

At Tecric I wrote 4 blogs to help increase there engagement and website visibility.
How did I do it? Compiled a list of 10 topics related to web-design, business, and tech space. Out of those, I selected 4 topics which cover CMS softwares, growing your business, and branding. I divided these into 3 tasks:
1. Collect as much research material as possible on each topic through articles, blogs and research papers.
2. Write as much as I can on each topic (Forget about corrections at this stage).
3. Proofread and edit the content to ensure its accuracy and readability.
